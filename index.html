DiffCartoon: Enhancing Access to Cartoon Animation Creation through Real-Time AI-Assisted Interaction

Anonymous Author(s)*,

Abstract

Animation creation bridges imaginative storytelling with expressive visual content. Interactivity is the core of this creative endeavor. Creators continuously engage in a dialogue between abstract imagination and visual expression to bring characters, worlds, and narratives to life. To truly unlock the expressive power of cartoons, this interaction must be in real-time. However, existing tools make such interaction costly. Traditional pipelines require expert skills and professional software. While recent advances in generative AI have lowered technical barriers, long feedback delays hinder the flow of interaction. Their limited narrative capabilities prevent non-expert users from turning early-stage ideas into cohesive animated stories. To bring real-time interaction into animation creation and make the process accessible to non-experts, we present DiffCartoon. Powered by a customized two-stage diffusion model that we propose, DiffCartoon enables cinematic 24fps video generation with fine-grained control over visual elements. Built around this innovative algorithm, DiffCartoon also integrates a domain-specific large language model (LLM) and a human-centered creative pipeline. This integration supports an interactive loop, allowing users to shape characters and narratives from rough ideas, enhancing creative engagement throughout the process. A user study with 14 non-professional creators demonstrates that DiffCartoon enables a seamless and engaging creative experience, lowering the barrier between imagination and animated expression.
